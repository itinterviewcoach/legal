## Payment

Payments are processed securely via **Paddle** (Merchant of Record). We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Paddle handles VAT/tax collection for all jurisdictions — you are charged the correct amount for your country automatically.

## Free Trial

New users automatically receive a **7-day free trial** of the Basic plan. No payment required to start. Cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid charges.

## Cancellation

Cancel your subscription at any time through the payment portal. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

## Refunds

Refunds are available within 7 days of payment if you have used fewer than 3 sessions. Contact itinterviewcoach@outlook.com to request a refund.

Full details: [Terms of Service](https://itinterviewcoach.github.io/legal/terms-of-service)
