## Payment

Two payment methods are available:

**Telegram Stars (⭐)** — Telegram's native payment system. Stars can be purchased in Telegram via App Store, Google Play, or in-app top-up. Works worldwide including Russia.

**Card (EUR) via Dodo Payments** — international credit/debit cards. Available at [store.dodopayments.com/interviewcoach](https://store.dodopayments.com/interviewcoach). Dodo Payments acts as Merchant of Record and handles VAT automatically.

## Free Trial

New users automatically receive a **7-day free trial** of the Basic plan. No payment required to start. Cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid charges.

## Cancellation

Cancel your subscription at any time through the payment portal. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

## Refunds

Refunds are available within 7 days of payment if you have used fewer than 3 sessions. Contact [itinterviewcoach@outlook.com](mailto:itinterviewcoach@outlook.com) to request a refund.

Full details: [Terms of Service](https://itinterviewcoach.github.io/legal/terms-of-service)
